About Me


Hi, I'm Aswini

A compassionate human who loves to blend technology and captivating narratives to create something truly special.

Stories inspire and connect people on a deep level.
Motherhood, resilience, and a growth mindset have played a profound role in shaping who I am today.

I'm an avid reader, writer, thinker and a keen learner.
Writing comes to me naturally. I have enjoyed creative writing ever since I was a kid. But content writing was not my first job.

Curious to know more about my journey? Scroll down for a sneak peak into my professional journey.

2009-2010: Software Developer

Ever since my school days, I had an undeniable love for reading, writing and a deep fascination with computers. Following my high school, I eagerly pursued a computer course and eventually completed my Master's degree. It helped kickstart my career as a software developer.

A majority of my technical expertise was acquired on the job through hands-on experience. My enthusiasm for technology and my fundamentals played a significant role in my ability to learn and adapt to new concepts effortlessly.It also allowed me to analyze technical issues and seek out the right solutions on time. I got accustomed to teamwork, meetings, requirement gathering processes, problem-solving, and multitasking, shaping me into a well-rounded professional.


2011-2012: Content Writer

I took a career break for family reasons. Meanwhile, I decided to leverage my love for writing. That's how I transitioned into the world of content creation. This opened doors to exciting opportunities, introduced me to doing research, developing content, and implementing SEO strategies.

I started crafting informative content, ensuring its uniqueness and relevance. This experience not only honed my writing skills but also instilled a profound understanding of diverse industries, their products, and services. I gained expertise in optimizing content for SEO purposes, strategically placing keywords, and skillfully paraphrasing copy to meet specific word counts. The freedom to work as a freelancer while traveling allowed me to experience different cultures and expand my horizons.


2013-2014: Elementary, ICT Teacher

As much as I enjoyed writing, I realized I was stuck with a monotonous writing style! I saw no scope for advancement. I felt the need for personal and professional growth. I craved new challenges, conversations, social interaction, and wanted to step out of my comfort zone.

With a Bachelor's degree in Education, I embraced the opportunity to impart knowledge, communicate effectively, and lead with confidence. I had the opportunity of teaching elementary graders in a new country for a year. It was a proud and an enriching experience that enhanced my adaptability and empathy. This remarkable journey of teaching not only refined my speaking and leadership skills but also instilled within me a profound sense of fulfillment.
